About

Our Story

Hagios School of Life was founded in 2011 by Ps. Samuel Julianto Suwondo, Pastor of Hagios Family - Pentecostal Church in Indonesia. It provides educational services for children of Pre-School level, Kindergarten level, Primary level and Secondary Level. The school aims to equip students with knowledge, Biblical wisdom, and characters of Christ, in doing so training them to live their utmost for God's glory and preparing them to enter tertiary education, employment, and entrepreneurship.

Vision

To equip students with knowledge, Biblical wisdom, and Christlike character.

Mission

1. To train students to live for God's glory through Christlike character training.
2. To equip students with knowledge needed to reach academic competence
set by the Indonesian government.
3. To train students to enter tertiary education, employment, and entrepreneurship.
